Selection Policy Selection Policy : Favour Fittest
A new generation is created following the steps below:
1. The individuals are sorted by fitness.
2. The 5 fittest individuals are copied to the next generation. No mutation is applied.
3. 5 random individuals are added to the next generation.
4. The 25 fittest individuals reproduce itself with another random individual of the current population until the next generation has 50 individuals.
Whenever the size of the next generation has reached the maximum of 50, the process gets interrupted and the subsequent steps are not performed.

- population size: 50
- wildcard rate: 10.0 % absolute: 5
The percentage of the fittest individuals taken for the next generation.
- random rate: 10.0 % absolute: 5
The percentage of newly generated individuals in the next generation.
- reproduction rate: 50.0 % absolute: 25
The percentage of the fittest individuals that are choosen to reproduce itself.
