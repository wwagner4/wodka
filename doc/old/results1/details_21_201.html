A language much like the one used for generating turtle graphics.
It consists of two commands.
Turn: Turns the turtle for a certain angle at the current position.
Forward: Moves the turtle for a certain amount. The max of that amount is 50. Negative values move it backwards.
The initial position of the turtle is in the center of the building area.
The building area has a size of 256x256 pixels.
If the turtle tryes to move out of the building area it is stopped at the border.

Selection Policy Selection Policy : Favour Fittest
A new generation is created following the steps below:
1. The individuals are sorted by fitness.
2. The 20 fittest individuals are copied to the next generation. No mutation is applied.
3. 10 random individuals are added to the next generation.
4. The 50 fittest individuals reproduce itself with another random individual of the current population until the next generation has 100 individuals.
Whenever the size of the next generation has reached the maximum of 100, the process gets interrupted and the subsequent steps are not performed.
Parameters:
- population size: 100
- wildcard rate: 20.0 % absolute: 20
The percentage of the fittest individuals taken for the next generation.
- random rate: 10.0 % absolute: 10
The percentage of newly generated individuals in the next generation.
- reproduction rate: 50.0 % absolute: 50
The percentage of the fittest individuals that are choosen to reproduce itself.
